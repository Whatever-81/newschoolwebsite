St. Ludington School

St. Ludington is a safe and affordable boarding school for ages 5-14. Here your child's education is valued and prized. Earning more than 5 awards for our extracurricular activities and electives, we provide Drama and Theatre, Art, Computer Science, Applied Engineering, Creative Writing, Music, Orchestra, Band, Choir, Python, PE, Dance, and more. Each child is assigned a bunk mate, who has had experience with our school before, so kids can make friends and get more comfortable with socializing. Our school field covers an outstanding 10000 square meters! It includes two playgrounds, for both the little kids and the older ones. We have a swimming pool and dance studio. Our teachers and staff will take good care of the students and help them thrive and learn. For more information, please go to - ludingtonlions@gmail.com

Campus Life

School Church



School Church

The School Church is a place filled with cathedrals and glass-stained pictures. We allow the children to follow whatever religion they choose. Our school church has lots of treasures and we do fun activities and prayers every Sunday. It is truly beautiful and is an awe-inspiring place.

School Field



School Field

All the children love to play at our school fields. They are lush and filled with grass and nature, and not to mention, we also have a swimming pool. There are two playgrounds, one for the younger kids and the other for the older ones. Our lunch monitor comes up with fun games for everyone to play as a class.

School Campus





School Campus

Every child has a locker, and a locker buddy. A locker buddy is a person who has already been around our school. He or she helps the new students find their way and helps them socialize with others. There are seperate classrooms for each class, all in an L shape. This way it is easier for each student to find their way.
